Viral Marketing Tool That Gets The Customer To Promote Your Product In Exchange For A Discount.

== Description ==

A viral marketing tool that lets your clients share information about your products and services within their social media profiles. UTail drives more sales, improves SEO and makes your business go viral within your targeted audience.

The Utail widget is perfect for any ecommerce item or entertainment platform. It can provide either a discount for purchase or a bonus.

== Installation ==

1. Install the plugin using Wordpress Plugins -> Add New button
2. Activate the plugin
3. Place `<?php do_shortcode('[utail-button]'); ?>` in your templates or just use [utail-button] in you pages or posts
